# Problem Definition: Consumer Shopping Trends 2026

## 1. Business Question
Which shopping preferences (Online, Store, Hybrid) dominate across different demographic segments (Age, Income), and how does total monthly spend vary between these groups?

## 2. Target Audience
Business Stakeholders (Non-technical focus on actionable trends).

## 3. Success Criteria
Identification of the preferred shopping type per segment to inform marketing and inventory strategies.

## 4. Target Variable
`shopping_preference` (Online, Store, Hybrid).

## 5. Key Metrics
- Total Monthly Spend (Online + Store)
- Monthly Online Orders
- Tech Savvy Score vs. Preference
- Income vs. Spending Power
